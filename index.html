Our Story

R&L Bitez was born from a simple idea: bring premium quality street food to Marapyane. What started as a small venture has grown into a local favorite, known for our commitment to quality ingredients and authentic flavors.

We believe great food should be accessible to everyone. That's why we source locally where possible and prepare every dish with the same care we would for our own family. From our signature burgers to our loaded fries, every item on our menu tells a story of passion and dedication.

When you choose R&L Bitez, you're not just getting a meal - you're experiencing what happens when street food meets premium quality, all crafted for the Kasi spirit.
