Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about ordering from R&L Bitez

How do I place an order? +

You can order by calling us at 082 421 8780, sending us a WhatsApp message to the same number, or visiting us at our location in Marapyane. For larger orders, we recommend calling ahead.

Do you deliver? +

We currently offer pickup service at our location in Marapyane. For special arrangements within Marapyane, please call us at 082 421 8780 to discuss options.

What payment methods do you accept? +

We accept cash, card payments (Visa/MasterCard), and mobile payments. All card transactions are secure and protected.

Can I customize my order? +

Absolutely! We're happy to accommodate special requests. Please let us know about any preferences when placing your order. Our burgers, loaded fries, and wings can all be customized to your liking.
